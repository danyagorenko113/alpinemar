<h2>What Is Payroll Compliance?</h2>
<p>Payroll compliance involves meeting legal obligations tied to paying employees, including adhering to payroll tax regulations, filing payroll taxes on time, and maintaining accurate records. Non-compliance can lead to significant financial risks, including audits and penalties. Alpine Mar's service ensures your payroll processes remain compliant with all applicable laws, reducing the risk of these costly issues.</p>

<h2>Davis-Bacon Act &amp; Prevailing Wage Compliance</h2>
<p>When managing payroll for federal and state-funded projects, compliance with regulations such as the Davis-Bacon Act and prevailing wage requirements is critical. These laws mandate that employees on public projects receive specific prevailing wages and benefits, which must be accurately tracked and reported. Non-compliance can lead to severe penalties, contract cancellations, and other legal repercussions.</p>
<p>Alpine Mar specializes in ensuring adherence to the Davis-Bacon Act, helping businesses manage the complexities of prevailing wage regulations. This includes tracking employee pay rates, benefits, and employment contracts to ensure all obligations are met, minimizing the risk of costly errors and potential project delays or terminations.</p>
<p>Our team also manages certified payroll reporting required by the Davis-Bacon Act, ensuring that all payroll data is properly documented and submitted for public projects. By verifying prevailing pay rates, job classifications, hours worked daily and weekly, and amounts paid, we help businesses navigate these intricate requirements, keeping projects on track.</p>
