<p>Alpine Mar IT provides AI security services that allow your organization to leverage artificial intelligence safely while reducing exposure to data risks and security gaps.</p>
<h3>What We Do</h3>
<p>Our AI security policy combines governance, risk management, and technical controls to protect sensitive data and reduce exposure to emerging threats.</p>
<ul>
<li>Assess how AI tools are currently being used across your organization and identify where sensitive data may be at risk</li>
<li>Build policies and technical controls around acceptable AI use that protect your data without shutting down productivity</li>
<li>Set up private AI instances and data masking layers that keep your business information from being shared with or used to train external AI models</li>
<li>Evaluate third-party AI integrations for security risks before they are connected to your systems</li>
<li>Monitor for AI-related data exposure and flag potential issues before they become reportable incidents</li>
</ul>
