<p>Alpine Mar IT puts multiple layers of protection between your team and the email threats landing in their inboxes every day. We filter out the dangerous messages before they arrive, use threat intelligence to catch the ones that slip through, and train your team to recognize the ones that still make their way in.</p>
<h3>What We Do</h3>
<ul>
<li>Advanced email filtering that blocks spam, phishing attempts, and malicious attachments before they reach your team</li>
<li>Anti-spoofing controls that protect your domain from being impersonated in outbound attacks</li>
<li>Real-world phishing simulations that test your team with realistic attack scenarios and identify who needs additional training</li>
<li>Ongoing security awareness training that keeps your staff sharp and up to date on the latest email-based threats</li>
<li>Monitoring and alerting for unusual email activity including compromised accounts sending suspicious messages</li>
</ul>
<h3>Why Alpine Mar IT</h3>
<p>Email security is not a set-it-and-forget-it problem. Attackers constantly update their tactics to get past filters and fool recipients. We stay ahead of those changes, updating your protections and keeping your team trained so your email environment stays as secure as possible without slowing anyone down.</p>
<p>Our email security services integrate seamlessly with platforms like Google Workspace and Microsoft 365 to provide comprehensive protection without disrupting daily work.</p>
