<p>Alpine Mar IT helps businesses build and maintain IT environments that satisfy regulatory requirements, pass audits, and hold up under scrutiny. We work across a broad range of frameworks including HIPAA, SOC 2, GDPR, CMMC, FERPA, FIPA, and WISP, and we do the actual technical work rather than advising from the sidelines.</p>
<h3>What We Do</h3>
<ul>
<li>Gap analysis against specific regulatory frameworks to identify exactly where your current IT controls fall short and what needs to change</li>
<li>Hands-on remediation by our engineers, who go directly into your cloud environments, servers, and systems to make the changes required to meet applicable standards</li>
<li>Policy development for access control, data retention, incident response, and other required documentation, configured to be technically enforced rather than just written down</li>
<li>Vendor and subcontractor vetting to make sure third parties connected to your environment do not create compliance liability for you</li>
<li>Continuous compliance monitoring with real-time visibility into your status, rather than the once-a-year scramble most businesses settle for</li>
<li>Support for your existing compliance team, or a fully outsourced compliance function for businesses that do not have one</li>
</ul>
<h3>Why Alpine Mar IT</h3>
<p>Most compliance consultants hand you a list of things to fix and send you a bill. We actually fix them. Our engineers roll up their sleeves and make the technical changes, write the code, and configure the systems so your compliance posture is real and defensible, not just documented.</p>
<p>Compliance does not end after an audit. We keep monitoring your environment year-round so you are never caught off guard when the next review comes around.</p>
