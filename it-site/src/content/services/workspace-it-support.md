<p>This is the day-to-day support most people think of when they think "IT," and it's also an ongoing relationship. We start by setting you up with the right system for how your team actually works, not a generic package, so you're not paying for tools you don't need or missing ones you do. From there, you have a team you can call when something breaks, not a vendor you have to re-explain your setup to every time. Setting up new computers, connecting printers and copiers, fixing whatever's broken in Word or Excel, helping someone get back into their account. You reach out, we handle it, usually remotely and usually fast.</p>
<p>We also keep a pre-approved list of hardware we already know plays well together, so when you need a new scanner or a new set of monitors, you're not guessing or running to a store. You tell us what you need, we source it and make sure it works with everything else you're running.</p>
<p>Behind the scenes, we're also running the same cybersecurity tools on your systems that keep everything monitored and protected, so support and security aren't two separate conversations or two separate bills. They're one relationship, one team, one system.</p>
