<p>Alpine Mar IT provides ongoing network security monitoring services that keep a continuous eye on what is happening across your environment and respond immediately when something looks wrong.</p>
<h3>What We Do</h3>
<p>Our approach integrates real-time network monitoring with proactive threat detection to identify suspicious activity early and reduce risk across your infrastructure.</p>
<ul>
<li>Continuous monitoring of network traffic for anomalies, unauthorized access attempts, and signs of active intrusion</li>
<li>Detection and alerting for unusual activity including unexpected outbound connections, lateral movement, and suspicious login patterns</li>
<li>Integration with your broader security posture including endpoint protection and incident response capabilities</li>
<li>Regular reporting on network activity and security events so you have a clear picture of your environment’s health</li>
<li>Rapid response when threats are detected to isolate and neutralize them before they can cause broader damage</li>
</ul>
<h3>Why Alpine Mar IT</h3>
<p>Threats on a network often do not announce themselves loudly. They move slowly, quietly, and deliberately. The only way to catch them is to be watching. We are always watching, combining expert analysis with advanced tools to enable early detection and faster response to potential security issues before they impact your business.</p>
