<p>Alpine Mar IT provides expert incident response services for businesses that need immediate, experienced help during a security incident or other active cyber threat. We move fast, communicate clearly, and do not leave until the threat is contained, the damage is understood, and your defenses are stronger than they were before.</p>
<h3>What We Do</h3>
<p>Our incident response capabilities are built to contain cyber incidents quickly, investigate the root cause, and strengthen your environment against future incidents.</p>
<ul>
<li>Rapid analysis and containment of active threats on your network and devices, isolating affected systems before the damage can spread to reduce business disruption</li>
<li>Thorough forensic investigation to understand exactly what happened, how the attacker got in, what they accessed, and what they left behind</li>
<li>Detailed incident reports that document the full timeline, root cause, and impact of the breach for your records, your insurers, and any regulatory requirements</li>
<li>Hardening recommendations based on the specific cause of the incident, with our engineers making the actual changes rather than handing you a checklist</li>
<li>Guidance on cybersecurity insurance, including working directly with your insurer on your behalf to navigate the claims process</li>
<li>Support drafting and managing communications to staff, board members, stakeholders, or affected customers regarding the incident</li>
</ul>
