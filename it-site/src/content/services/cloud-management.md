<p>Alpine Mar IT delivers cloud management services across the complete lifecycle of your cloud environment, from initial setup and cloud migration to ongoing management, security, and cost optimization. We make sure your cloud investment is working as hard as it should be.</p>
<p>Our team supports modern cloud infrastructure across Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, and other cloud platforms to help companies manage resources efficiently and scale with confidence.</p>
<h3>What We Do</h3>
<p>Alpine Mar IT’s cloud management solutions are designed to optimize performance, enhance security, and provide measurable cost savings across your cloud operations.</p>
<ul>
<li>Setup and secure configuration of new cloud environments including Google Workspace and Microsoft 365</li>
<li>Cost and feature analysis to make sure your licensing matches your actual usage and your team has access to the tools they need</li>
<li>Migration of legacy and on-premise applications into the cloud for better security, accessibility, and long-term maintainability</li>
<li>Ongoing management of users, groups, security settings, and sharing permissions</li>
<li>Monitoring for suspicious logins, unauthorized access, and unusual account activity</li>
<li>Regular reviews of your cloud configuration to catch misconfigurations, over-permissioned accounts, and unnecessary access</li>
</ul>
<h3>Why Alpine Mar IT</h3>
<p>Cloud management is not glamorous, but it matters enormously. A poorly configured Google Workspace or Microsoft 365 environment is one of the most common sources of both security incidents and unnecessary spending. We keep your cloud environment clean, current, and cost-effective by improving cloud usage, strengthening access control, and helping you reduce unnecessary cloud costs.</p>
