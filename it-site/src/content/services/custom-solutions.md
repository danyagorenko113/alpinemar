<p>Every so often a request comes in that doesn't fit cybersecurity or day-to-day support. Maybe it's an old server that's core to how you operate and can't just be replaced. Maybe it's a custom app, or a system that needs to talk to something unusual.</p>
<p>When that happens, we don't pretend it's a standard job. We scope it, bring in the right specialists for that specific project, and give you a clear quote before any work starts. It costs more than our standard plans, because it's genuinely custom work, but it means you get people who actually know that particular problem instead of a generalist guessing their way through it.</p>
