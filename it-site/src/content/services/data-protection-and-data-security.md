<p>Your business data is one of your most valuable assets and one of your most significant liabilities if it falls into the wrong hands or disappears without warning.</p>
<p>Data protection extends well beyond simple backups. Comprehensive data protection and security services maintain your data's safety, accessibility, and regulatory compliance throughout its entire lifecycle.</p>
<p>Alpine Mar IT provides end-to-end data management, spanning automated backups and encrypted storage through access auditing and sensitive data monitoring. The company ensures data remains protected against loss, unauthorized access, and unintended exposure while minimizing team burden. Core objectives include preventing breaches, safeguarding customer information, and strengthening security posture across infrastructure.</p>
<h2>What We Do</h2>
<p>Data protection services are engineered to prevent data loss, secure sensitive information, and enable rapid business recovery following incidents.</p>
<ul>
<li>Automated, encrypted backup management for Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, servers, and individual devices</li>
<li>Multiple redundant backup points that eliminate single points of failure and enable quick recovery from any scenario</li>
<li>Automated data archiving and secure deletion processes aligned with legal retention requirements</li>
<li>Scheduled audits of user permissions for shared folders and documents ensuring appropriate access controls</li>
<li>Continuous monitoring for personally identifiable information, credit card numbers, bank account details, and other sensitive data in email, documents, and chat platforms</li>
<li>Controls preventing sensitive files from uploading to personal drives, copying to USB devices, or reaching unauthorized external addresses</li>
</ul>
<h2>Why Alpine Mar IT</h2>
<p>Data breaches and data loss typically provide no advance warning. Organizations recovering swiftly are those with proper preventative systems established beforehand. The company builds and maintains these systems so businesses resume operations within hours rather than weeks if incidents occur.</p>
<p>Alpine Mar IT maintains vigilant oversight of data movement and access permissions, identifying exposure risks before they escalate into reportable incidents. These security services help organizations protect personal data, maintain data protection law compliance, and remain ahead of emerging threats.</p>
