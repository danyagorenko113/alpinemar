<p>This is an ongoing relationship, not a one-time project. If your business runs on Google Workspace or Microsoft 365 (and most do), we become an admin inside that environment and stay there. We're not swooping in after something breaks, we're already logged in, already watching, every day you're a client.</p>
<p>What that looks like in practice: we set you up on a monthly plan, put our monitoring tools in place, and start flagging anything that looks off, a login from a country you've never visited, a password reset you didn't request, a file suddenly shared outside your company. You'll also move to passwordless login, so getting into your own accounts gets easier, not harder.</p>
<p>When something does go wrong, you're not calling a different company for incident response. It's the same team, the same access, the same tools already in place. We're already inside your environment, so we're already moving.</p>
<p>Day to day, you'll have a dashboard you can actually read, with an AI built in that you can ask questions like "are we secure right now" and get a straight answer. When your insurance company or a client asks if you're compliant, you'll have that answer ready instead of a scramble.</p>
