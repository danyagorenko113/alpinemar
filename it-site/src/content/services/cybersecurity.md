<p>Alpine Mar IT delivers comprehensive cybersecurity services built for businesses that need enterprise-grade protection without the enterprise-sized IT department. We monitor your environment around the clock, respond to threats before they escalate, and train your team to be the first line of defense rather than the weakest link. Our team concentrates on strengthening your overall security posture, identifying security gaps, and helping you manage risk across your entire environment.</p>
<p>From endpoint protection and email filtering to phishing simulations and incident response, we handle the full spectrum of cybersecurity so you can focus on running your business.</p>
<h3>What We Do</h3>
<p>Our cybersecurity solutions are prepared to detect threats early, protect sensitive data, and support business operations without disruption.</p>
<ul>
<li>24/7 managed detection and response that monitors your environment continuously and intervenes before incidents become crises</li>
<li>Endpoint and device protection including virus scanning, software patch management, and device security hardening</li>
<li>Email filtering and anti-phishing protection that stops malicious messages before they ever reach your team</li>
<li>Real-world phishing simulations combined with staff training to build lasting security awareness across your organization</li>
<li>Rapid, expert incident response with full investigation, analysis, and a clear path to recovery and hardening</li>
</ul>
